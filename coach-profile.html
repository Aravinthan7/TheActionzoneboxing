OUR Trainer

Mahendhra Kumar

Muay Thai Coach ,Strength , Conditioning and Nutrition Specialist

With over 15 years of experience in Muay Thai, Mahendrakumar is a highly accomplished fighter and coach. He is a Multiple -time national gold medalist and has won multiple international gold medals, showcasing his exceptional skills and dedication to the sport. Mahendrakumar holds a Master’s degree in Strength and Conditioning and Muaythai boxing graduated in Thailand and completed Muaythai instructor level 2 coach certification which complements his extensive practical experience. His academic background enables him to design comprehensive training programs that enhance both physical performance and technical proficiency.



Specializations

  • Advanced Muay Thai techniques.
  • Strength and conditioning programs.
  • Personalized training plans.
  • Mental toughness and fight strategy.


Achievements

  • Multiple National Gold Medals in Muay Thai.
  • Multiple International Gold Medals.
  • Professional Fight Winner on the international stage


Coaching Philosophy

Mahendrakumar believes in a holistic approach to training, focusing on both the physical and mental aspects of Muay Thai. His sessions are tailored to meet the individual needs of each athlete, ensuring they reach their full potential. He emphasizes technique, strategy, and conditioning, creating a balanced and effective training regimen.



Why Train with Mahendrakumar?

Training with Mahendrakumar means learning from one of the best in the field. His proven track record, both as a competitor and a coach, speaks volumes about his expertise and commitment. Whether you’re a beginner looking to learn the basics or an advanced fighter aiming to compete at the highest levels, Mahendrakumar’s guidance will help you achieve your goals.
